Watermelon Chiffon Pie

Servings: 1 9inch pie
3 cup watermelon Blend Strain Add 1/2 cup to gelatin
let sit 2 minutes		 Boil Remove heat Add rest of watermelon Chill
till firmish		 Fold in Fold in Pour in crust
1 envelope of gelatin
juice of 1 lime
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream Beat to stiff peaks
3/4 cup powdered sugar
1 egg white Beat to stiff peaks
1 x Graham Cracker Crust
