Pumpkin Roll

Servings: 1 roll
Cake
3/4 cup flour Mix spread over 9x13 pan
on parchment paper		 Bake at 375F for 15 minutes Roll up and wrap in towel
for 20 minutes
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1 cup pumpkin
3 eggs
1 teaspoon lemon juice
Filling
8oz cream cheese, soft Mix
1/4 cup butter, soft
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup powdered sugar
