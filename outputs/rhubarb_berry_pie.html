Rhubarb Berry Pie

Servings: 1 9inch pie
3/4 cup sugar Mix Add Stir gentrly Let sit for 15 minutes Place in crust
add latice top		 Bake at 375F
for 25 minutes with foil
20-35 more
till top is golden and bubbly
3 tablespoon quick cooking tapioca
2 cups sliced rhubarb
2 cups berries
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
dash nutmeg
